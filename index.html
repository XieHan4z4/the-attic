The Attic

"In the noise, find peace; in the stillness, find memories."

The Attic is an interactive audio experience that explores the contrast between modern anxiety and nostalgic peace. Set in a dimly lit environment, players rely primarily on their auditory senses to navigate and reconstruct fragmented pasts.

1. The Experience (Demo)

A full walkthrough showing the transition from movement noise to the clarity of finding a memory object.

2. Sound Design

The sounds of childhood objects were carefully mixed to sound slightly muffled, representing the grainy nature of old memories. Only when standing perfectly still does the static noise fade.

Audio 1: The Wooden Train (Raw vs. Mixed)

Audio 2: Global Static Noise (Movement)

3. Technical Implementation

Using Unreal Engine 5 to track player speed and spatial distance, and Max/MSP to control the audio mixing dynamically, the project creates an auditory radar system.

UE5 Blueprint: Tracking speed and distance via OSC
Max/MSP Patcher: Receiving OSC data and dynamically adjusting volume levels.
DAW Session: Pre-processing the memory fragments to fit the "attic" acoustic space.
